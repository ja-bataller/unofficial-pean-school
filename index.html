History

The school which was formerly known as Pean Children’s House, was first established in 1978 as a tutorial center up until 1985. In 1987, it started its Pre School level. In 1999, the Department of Education, Culture and Sports permitted Pean Children’s House to operate its Elementary Levels, with school’s name changed to Pean Academy. In 2013, the founder Angelina Yuzon Santos gave the full management of the school to her children.

The new management decided to offer new services and the name was changed to Pean Integrated School of Paranaque, Inc. (PISP). At present, PISP is continuing to expand its facilities well as improve and update its school programs. The effectiveness of our academic programs may be seen from the high percentile rankings of former students entering various exclusive schools and scholarship grants.
